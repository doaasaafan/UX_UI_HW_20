Project Summary

"The Hope For Children Foundation" is a not-for-profit, a top children charitable organization.This top rated charity, was created on April 2, 1998 to aid sexually and physically abused children, adults and their protectors.

Project Goal

We redesiged the "Hope for Children" website to allow for better organization of their content's website information as well as create an appealing aesthetic of the website to increase user engagement for donations.

Details

How long did it take ?

Designing: 3 weeks

My Role:
Researched and designed

UX designer

The Problem and Our Solution

THE PROBLEM

The foundation doesn't get enough donations, they need to update their website to have more donations and also get Congress's attention regarding the alarming statistics of child abuse in America.

OUR SOLUTION

Redesign the Homepage to allow for better organization of their content's website information as well as create an appealing aesthetic of the website to increase user engagement for donations.

Redesign the flow and secondary pages of their “Donate” section from the primary navigation menu.

Consolidating some of the secondary donate pages as well as make their donation process a simpler navigation

The accessibility to “contact Us” to allow for better interaction with potential donors and clients.

The User Research Process

Interviews

Interview with the Hope for Children Foundation's executive director to better understand their mission and goals. Their main mission was to have their website reflect a more visible call to action item to get Congress’s attention regarding the alarming statistics of child abuse in America.

Surveys

Survey sent out to class on child abuse to gain insight on key points. Users felt would be more helpful to be displayed on a website focused on awareness on child abuse for the public.

The Define Process

User Persona

Based on the interview and the survey results we conducted a user persona. Stacy a social worker, who Stacy has been working as a social worker for 5 years, specializing in working with victims of domestic and sexual abuse. She is passionate about helping those in need and making a difference in their lives. she wants a high number of petition signatures to provoke legal change, She wants to raise awareness towards child abuse through statistics, warning signs, and educating the public on how to report suspected child abuse.

Affinity Diagram

We conducted the Affinity diagram and categorized it to be able to gather and organize all the feedback and what the user need to use the website. also what will make it easy for him to make a petition and get the information he need. we put all the feedback under 7 categories, the categories are the goals, donations, petition signing, most importatnt resourses needed for non profit website about child abuse,famililarity to use non profit websites and famililarty with local child abuse laws.

The Ideation Process

Site Map Iteration

Based on the user research process and feedback we got we iterated the sitemap to fulfill the user needs and help the foundation to get their needs from their site.

User Flow

We iterated the User Flow to be more easy to use, esure the user needs, increase the donations as well as signing a petition and also updated the contact me page.
